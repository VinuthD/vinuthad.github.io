About

Two disciplines, one mindset

Final-year AI/ML engineering student in Karnataka, building a bridge between how machines reason and how people feel using a product.

I'm an AI/ML engineering student who spent the last two years building models that read X-rays of railway tracks, transcribe lecture videos, and filter spam. Somewhere in that process, I realised the hardest part was never the model — it was making the output make sense to a human.

That's what pulled me toward Figma, prototyping, and interface design. I care about the same thing in both worlds: reducing noise until only the useful signal is left.

Right now I'm looking for an internship or junior role where I can keep learning UX fundamentals while bringing a technical, data-literate perspective to product teams.
